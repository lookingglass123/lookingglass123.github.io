Welcome to Looking Glass

We are an interdisciplinary neurotechnology group with passionate neuroscientists, programmers, and engineers. We share a common enthusiasm for making cool stuff with brains and tech!

We are collecting behavioral data using EEG. Looking glass allows us to discover if a certain person knows other faces or not.
